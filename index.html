River's assessment

  • This prototype is the pixel producer. Our LED grid is exactly the PIXEL_FRAME payload (RGBA8, row-major, top-left) — the Output window previews that frame 1:1.
  • This build models the real fabrication: a 24′ wall of 3 abutted 8′×4′ panels behind one continuous 134×19 = 2,546 dome field on a 2″ grid — 14 of the 2,560 supply spare. Logically it's still a dense W×H grid; read W/H from HELLO, don't hardcode.
  • Button presses are the reverse channel (BUTTON_STATE). Our click-to-cycle stands in for it until the socket exists.
  • Before hardware: honour ≤30 Hz, expect wall→black on disconnect (no persistence), and treat gamma + brightness-clamp as unresolved — don't ship smooth fades assuming linear PWM. Only one producer can push pixels.
